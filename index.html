ABOUT

        Hi! I'm Marina. I'm a recent college graduate looking to use my computing and analysis skills for social change. I am most interested in the fields of healthcare, environmental justice, and education.
        When I started college I was very excited to learn more about the environment and how we were affecting it. I immediately joined the Office of Sustainability as a fellow and was introduced to the idea of holistic sustainability. This view of sustainability necessitates a balance of and consideration for environmental stewardship, social justice, and economic viability. This made sense to me. Of course no solution could be long lasting without taking all of these spheres into consideration! Through this view of sustainability I found my true passion for social justice. I am interested in how environmental degradation disproportionately affects certain people and therefore becomes a social issue. Healthcare and education are similarly important to our communities and also require equity and inclusion. I am excited to work towards these goals of just social structures by using my technical, social, and organizational skills.
